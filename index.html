Sheet 02 About

I'm an 18-year-old, first-year BS Civil Engineering student who spends as much time in dev tools and terminals as in drafting software. That dual grounding shapes how I build — structurally sound, deliberately tested, and built to hold weight.

Alongside my studies, I carry close to 10 years of leadership experience — from organizations at the school level up to community-level councils, coordinating people, documentation, and projects across multiple groups at once.

On the build side, I work with modern no-code and low-code tools alongside light hand-coding — HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — to ship sites that are fast and clean. On the testing side, I bring an ethical hacking mindset: checking for the weak points most freelancers never think to look for.

I'm entering freelancing to combine both — sites that don't just look good, but are built, led, and checked properly.
